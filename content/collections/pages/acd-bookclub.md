## ACD Book Club

The Adams County Democrats Book Club is an informal gathering for people who enjoy reading and talking about books related to democracy, civic life, history, politics, and social justice.

### How It Works

The book club meets regularly — in person when possible, and online when that works better for members. We choose books together, read on our own schedule, and then gather to discuss.

Anyone is welcome to join, whether or not you are a registered Democrat. The goal is good conversation and a deeper understanding of the world we all share.

### What We Read

Past and upcoming selections include books on:
- The history of American democracy
- Voting rights and civic participation
- Rural political identity
- Political philosophy and ethics
- Local and state history in Idaho
