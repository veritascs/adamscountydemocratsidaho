An important election is coming up on May 19. With several contested Democratic races this year, every vote matters to ensure the strongest candidates represent our values.

If you have questions about voting or need to register, visit the [Adams County Elections](https://www.co.adams.id.us/department/clerk-auditor-recorder/election-information) webpage. It includes answers and links to start registration online. For additional help, call (208) 253-4561 (Option 5) or visit the county office at 210 Industrial Avenue in Council.

Below is the list of candidates endorsed by the Adams County Central Committee. Thank you to everyone who participated in the endorsement workshop—your input was invaluable. These candidates received unanimous support and are considered our most competitive options. With growing dissatisfaction at both the state and federal levels, this election presents a real opportunity for change. Your vote matters now more than ever.
