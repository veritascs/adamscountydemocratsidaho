## Training and Workshops

The Adams County Democrats periodically offer workshops and training sessions to help members build the skills they need to be effective civic participants and advocates.

### What We Cover

**Canvassing and Voter Outreach**
Learn how to have productive conversations with neighbors, use canvassing apps, and represent the party positively in the community.

**Voter Registration Drives**
How to organize and run a voter registration event, including what is legally required and how to make it welcoming.

**Campaign Basics**
An introduction to how local campaigns work, from filing to Election Day. Useful whether you are thinking of running yourself or helping someone else.

**Meeting Facilitation**
How to run an effective, inclusive meeting — useful for anyone stepping into a leadership role or organizing community events.

**Digital and Social Media**
How to use social platforms responsibly and effectively to communicate local party news and events.
