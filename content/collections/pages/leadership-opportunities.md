## Leadership Opportunities

The Adams County Democratic Party is run by volunteers who step up to guide the organization, coordinate activities, and represent their neighbors.

### Open Leadership Roles

**County Chair**
The Chair leads the central committee, runs meetings, and serves as the public face of the county party. This role requires regular availability and strong communication skills.

**Vice Chair**
The Vice Chair supports the Chair and steps in when needed. This is a great entry point for someone interested in eventually leading the organization.

**Secretary**
The Secretary takes and maintains minutes for all official meetings, manages membership records, and handles official correspondence.

**Treasurer**
The Treasurer manages party finances, files required campaign finance reports with the state, and keeps the committee informed on the financial picture.

**Precinct Captain**
Precinct Captains organize outreach in their specific precinct — connecting with neighbors, helping people register to vote, and turning out voters during elections.

### How to Get Started

If you are interested in any of these roles or want to learn more about what they involve, reach out to the county chair. No experience is required — just a commitment to showing up and helping.
