## Our Mission

Adams County Democrats exist to build a more participatory, inclusive, and accountable county by supporting neighbors who want to make their community stronger.

### What We Believe

- Every resident of Adams County deserves a voice in decisions that affect their lives, regardless of how long they have lived here or how much money they have.
- Local government is where civic participation is most direct and most powerful. We work to lower the barriers to engagement for everyone.
- Public schools, rural healthcare, clean water, and responsible stewardship of public lands are not partisan issues — they are community needs that require honest attention.
- Democracy only works when people show up. We exist to help people show up.

### What We Are Working On Locally

1. Supporting strong public schools and the educators and families who depend on them.
2. Advocating for accessible rural healthcare and fighting for services that serve all residents, not just those closest to larger cities.
3. Protecting public lands, waterways, and the agricultural heritage that defines our county.
4. Encouraging transparent local government with open meetings, honest communication, and genuine opportunities for public input.
5. Building a welcoming, active local party that makes it easy for new people to get involved and stay involved.

### Our Commitment

We believe in honest, respectful conversations about the issues that matter in Adams County. We welcome any neighbor who wants to work toward a better community — whether you are new to civic life or a longtime participant.
